Early Yielded Eye Quality

EYEQ

EyeQ is an innovative AI-driven solution designed to revolutionize the early detection of diabetic retinopathy, addressing critical healthcare challenges in vision screening. Our cutting-edge technology aims to provide accessible, accurate, and cost-effective screening for individuals at risk of vision loss.

About us

Advanced AI Technology

Utilizes deep learning algorithms to analyze retinal images with high accuracy and sensitivity across various stages of diabetic retinopathy.

Scalable Healthcare Solution

Deployable in low-resource settings, enabling widespread access to critical eye health screening where trained professionals may be limited.

Comprehensive Data Analysis

Leverages comprehensive datasets to provide insights into disease progression, supporting both individual patient care and public health monitoring.

Multi-Purpose Application

Supports individual patient screening, pharmaceutical research, and population-level health tracking, making it a versatile tool in healthcare management.
