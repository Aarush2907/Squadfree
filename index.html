Model United Nations

THE MUN CLUB

A Model United Nations (MUN) club is an educational simulation where students role-play as delegates to the UN, engaging in diplomacy and debate to address global issues. This enriches their understanding of international relations and hones key skills like public speaking, negotiation, and leadership.
